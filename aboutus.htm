About Us

Founded in January 2009, we are an official alumni chapter of the Kappa Sigma Fraternity based in the greater San Francsico Bay Area. We strive to help local alumni brothers connect through social events, career networking, and other opportunities. We also are active with Kappa Sigma undergraduate chapters in the area.

Benefits to being an official Kappa Sigma Fraternity alumni chapter include the following:

  • Help local alumni brothers continue their Kappa Sigma involvement, and continue to enjoy the benefits of Kappa Sigma through social events, career networking, and other opportunities.
  • Become a founding member of an alumni chapter that will help hundreds of future alumni brothers stay involved in Kappa Sigma
  • Help establish new chapters or re-colonize dormant Kappa Sigma undergraduate chapters in the area
  • Have the alumni chapter listed on the Kappa Sigma website (www.kappasigma.org)
  • Nominate and recognize local alumni brothers through the John Tower Award program
  • Post listings to the Caduceus and other Kappa Sigma publications

If you are interested in becoming a member, please visit the membership section of the website.
