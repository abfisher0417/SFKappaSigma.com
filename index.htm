Welcome!

Welcome to the Kappa Sigma SF Bay Area Alumni Chapter website!

Started in January 2009, we are an official alumni chapter of the Kappa Sigma Fraternity based in the San Francsico Bay Area. Through social events, career networking, and other opportunities, we help local alumni brothers continue their Kappa Sigma involvement, and continue to enjoy the benefits of Kappa Sigma. We also particpate with and help out Kappa Sigma undergraduate chapters in the area.

Browse our website, attend an event, and consider becoming an active member of the Kappa Sigma SF Bay Area Alumni Chapter to help hundreds of future alumni brothers stay involved in Kappa Sigma.
